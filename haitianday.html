iTech recognizes hatian flag day as a schoolwide event

Published 5/18/2022 tag category 3 comments

On May 18th it is known in haiti as their flag day, when thier first flag was made after the indepence of haiti in janurary 1st, 1802. For many haitians they usually celebrate by branding their flag all over South Florida however for one highschool in the little haiti area known as iTech has decided to recognize the hatian population in their school by celebrating hatian flag day, the entrance ways were decorated with beatiful plants with ribbons and ballons matching the flags color scheme. at the end of the day there was a live performance of the island country's music.
